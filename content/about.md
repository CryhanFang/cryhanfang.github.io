# Han Fang (方瀚)

I am currently a Research Scientist at the [Xingchen AGI Lab, China Telecom Artificial Intelligence Technology (Beijing) Co., Ltd.](https://www.teleai.com.cn/), where I focus on developing generative AI and advancing its applications in downstream domains. As a core contributor, I led the research and development of the **'Xinghe' Vision Model series**, the text-to-image model **TeleImage-1.0**, and the multimodal large model **TeleMM-2.0**.

My research aims to bridge the gap between vision and language by enabling models to perform **vision-centric reasoning**, with a focus on long video understanding, free-form multimodal grounding, and vision-language pretraining.
